What does D2D mean in basketball?

The acronym "D2D", as used in basketball and other sports, stands for "Day-to-Day". It is a representation of a player's injury status. If a player is listed as "D2D" on the injury report, it means they are recovering from an injury and are close to returning to the team's game time lineup. In other words, their ability to play is being evaluated on a "day-to-day" basis (as opposed to being listed as "Out", where there is no need to consistently check their ability to return).
